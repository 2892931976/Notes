
# Use `adb screenrecord` to Capture Screen Video

#### Example:

    adb screenrecord –-size 640x480 –-bit-rate 3000000 /sdcard/my.mp4

#### Params:

    --size <WIDTHxHEIGHT>
    --bit-rate <RATE>

#### Stop Recording
You may press `ctrl + C` to stop recording.
